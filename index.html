FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jankalyan? +
Jankalyan is a free platform helping Indian citizens discover government schemes from Central and State governments. Search, check eligibility, read about benefits, and find how to apply — all in one place.
How many schemes are available? +
We cover 166+ government schemes, 94+ scholarships, and 40+ research fellowships from Central ministries and 25 States & UTs across India.
Is this an official government website? +
Jankalyan is an independent platform. All scheme information is sourced from official government websites like myScheme.gov.in and verified regularly. We provide direct links to official application portals.
How do I check my eligibility? +
Each scheme page lists detailed eligibility criteria including age, income, category, education, and other requirements. You can also use the filter tools on our Schemes page to discover schemes matching your profile.
Can I apply for schemes through Jankalyan? +
We provide step-by-step application guidance and direct links to official application portals. The actual application is submitted on the respective government website.
How is the information verified? +
Our team regularly updates scheme details from official government notifications, PIB releases, and ministry websites. Last updated dates are shown on each scheme page.
